# Instrument Backlog & Candidate List

These instruments were identified in the competitive analysis but are currently deferred to keep the engine focused and lightweight.

## I. Western Woodwinds
- **Recorder (竖笛)**: Simple, whistle-like.
- **Oboe (双簧管)**: Nasal, penetrating (Double reed).
- **Bassoon (大管)**: Deep, comical/staccato (Double reed).
- **Pan Flute (排箫)**: Breathy, percussive attack.
- **Bagpipes (苏格兰风笛)**: Drone + Chanter structure (Complex polyphony).

## II. Saxophone Family
- **Tenor Sax (次中音萨克斯)**: Lower, breathier than Alto.
- **Baritone Sax (上低音萨克斯)**: Deep, growling bass.

## III. Western Brass
- **French Horn (圆号)**: Mellow, majestic, wide range.
- **Tuba (大号)**: Deep brass bass.
- **Muted Trumpet (弱音小号)**: Miles Davis style, distinct metallic buzz.

## IV. Western Strings
- **Viola (中提琴)**: Darker, warmer than violin.
- **Double Bass (低音提琴)**: Very low, bowed or plucked.
- **Strings Ensemble (弦乐合奏)**: Polyphonic lush chords (Requires chord generation logic).
- **Pizzicato (拨奏)**: Short, plucked articulation.

## V. Ethnic / Traditional
- **Xiao (洞箫)**: Lower, deeper than Dizi, vertical blowing.
- **Hulusi (葫芦丝)**: Drone + Melody, extremely distinct soft reed sound.
- **Pipa (琵琶)**: Plucked, percussive, rapid tremolo (Requires discrete logic).
- **Morin Khuur (马头琴)**: Mongolian cello, deep, windy, harmonic-rich.

## VI. Keyboards & Compound
- **Accordion (手风琴)**: Breathing bellows, reed organ sound.
- **Glockenspiel (钢片琴)**: High pitched, bell-like metallic attack.
- **Harp (竖琴)**: Plucked, resonant, glissando-heavy.
